Reuben Sandwich

A reuben is a sandwich featuring corned beef on rye bread

Ingredients

  • 8 slices rye bread
  • 1/2 cup Thousand Island dressing
  • 8 slices swiss cheese
  • 8 slices deli sliced corned beef
  • 1 cup sauerkraut, drained
  • 2 tablespoons butter, softened

Steps

  1. Preheat a large griddle or skillet of medium heat
  2. Spread one side of bread slices even with Thousand Island dressing
  3. On four bread slices, layer one slice Swiss cheese, 2 slices corned beef, 1/4 cup sauerkraut, and a second slice of Swiss cheese
  4. Top with remaining bread slices, dressing down
  5. Butter the top of each sandwich
  6. Place sandwiches butter side down on preheated griddle
  7. Butter the tops of each sandwich
  8. Grill both sides are golden brown, about 5 minutes per side
  9. Serve Hot
